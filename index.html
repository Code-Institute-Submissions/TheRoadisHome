Why Choose Us?

Small groups

Join a small group of travellers that, like you, are eager to explore what our world has to offer. Our groups have no more than 16 people all sharing amazing, sometimes life-changing experiences. If you’re travelling alone, we won’t penalise you with single-person supplements.

Local guides

All of our tour guides are all locally based and are experts to the area. They are knowledgable, passionate and know the places you’ll be going like the back of their hand.

Support local communities

Rather than staying in multi-national hotel chains, we prefer to stay in locally-owned hotels and B&Bs. Our tour guides know all about the best ‘off the beaten track’ restaurants and bars where you’ll be able to fully immerse yourself into the local culture.

Flexibility

Our tours are a mixture of planned itineraries with the flexibility of doing your own thing. There’s lots of free time where you can choose to either book onto our optional activities or do some solo exploring, if you’d prefer.
