Our Tours

Western Canada

This is a 14 day adventure across Western Canada, where you’ll visit beautiful scenery in various National Parks, the Rocky Mountains, Whistler and Vancouver Island, amongst others! Explore what the West has to offer in its incredible mountain ranges, waterfalls and glacial lakes.

Western USA

From the casinos and glamour of Las Vegas, to the tranquility of Yosemite, this 14 day trip travels through some of the best national parks in the US. Camp under the stars at the Grand Canyon, walk across the famous Golden Gate Bridge in San Francisco and finish with some celeb-spotting in Los Angeles.

from £1300

Cuba

You may think you've gone back in time when you see the vintage cars on the roads in Havana. This colourful Caribbean island has so much to offer, from the white sand beaches and turquoise waters to the cobblestone streets and colonial architecture of Trinidad. You will see it all on this 8 day adventure.

from £1050

Spain & Portugal

Enjoy 14 days of sun, surf and Sangria. Get lost in the narrow streets of Barcelona's old town, sit by the river and try Porto's signature drink - Port or maybe catch a game of football at Real Madrid's Santiago Bernabéu Stadium.

from £1450
